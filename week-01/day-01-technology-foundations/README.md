# Week 1 Day 1 - Technology Foundations

## Problem
This assignment required me to explain foundational technology concepts that support cybersecurity and GRC work. The goal was to understand how computer systems, networking devices, storage, data centers, and cloud environments function, and why they matter in risk and compliance work.

## What I Did
I completed a written assignment covering:
- computer system basics
- input, processing, and output
- hardware vs software
- networking components such as modems, routers, and switches
- storage concepts including HDD, SSD, and cloud storage
- data centers and business importance
- cloud computing and service models such as IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS
- cloud deployment models such as public, private, and hybrid cloud
- GRC-related risks involving routers, data centers, and cloud environments
- business impact of infrastructure failure
- cloud migration security questions
- a one-page visual cheat sheet

## Key Learnings
From this project, I learned that:
- GRC professionals need a strong understanding of technical foundations
- infrastructure decisions create business risk, security risk, and compliance impact
- cloud environments require careful review of access, encryption, compliance, and shared responsibility
- documenting technical concepts clearly is important for audit, governance, and risk communication
